  • Don't know exactly how to use the sustaining pedal (延音踏板) in this piece 没有清晰的颗粒感
  • "Tempo di marcia": the sense of "marching" is not obvious; a little bit too fast. 进行曲的风格不强，速度稍快
  • Wrong keys, oops! 错音==
  • the interaction between left and right hand is weak; not enough accent for certain keys; cascades are not strong enough 左右手的互动、某些重音、层次感不够到位
  • not as light as Morath 还是有些厚重，不轻快灵动

Tips for recording

  • Protect your phone with a heavy object to avoid a catastrophe.
  • Connect your phone with a bluetooth recording device, for instance a wireless headphone, and put it away from the piano, so you can have a better sound quality.
  • Of course you can skip the above two tips by buying a smart phone holder!
